How It Works

ShareTracker gathers real-time data from the Finnhub API, providing users with up-to-the-minute stock prices, performance data, and stock analysis. Users can search for stocks, add them to their portfolio, and monitor changes in value over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a stock to my portfolio?

Simply search for the stock symbol in the search bar, enter the number of shares and the purchase price, and click "Add to Portfolio."

Is the stock data real-time?

Yes! We use the Finnhub API to ensure that all stock prices and market data are updated in real-time.
